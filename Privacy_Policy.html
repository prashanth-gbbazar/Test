Karnataka Political News Privacy Policy


This privacy policy explains how, why we collect volunteers information, our service providers information, and where we share and disclose your information. Your use of and access to the Services of Karnataka Political News app and website is subject to this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use as defined in both Karnataka Political News mobile app and Karnataka Political News website. By using the services or by registering us (Karnataka Political News app) your information, you have read, understood and agreed to the policies outlined in this page. We reserve the right to modify, update or change of the terms of this privacy policy at any time at its sole discretion.


Why Privacy policy is required?

It's a practice of being transparent & providing clear information towards service users(app and website) about what is happening with their personal data including gathering of data, usage of data, and storing of data that they are providing you with - knowingly and unknowingly.


Definitions

User means a person/volunteer who has signed up or requested with "Karnataka Political News" for the use the service . Partner means the party having sole responsibility for the emergency response service using the Karnataka Political News platform to connect with the User. Emergency Responder means the person, volunteer or third party who provides the emergency response to the User of app or website & which works on behalf of Karnataka Political News during the any kind of emergency.


Purposes to Process Your Personal Information

When you visit our (Karnataka Political News) Website OR Karnataka Political News App, we will collect your name, mobile number, email address at the time of registration. We will use your phone number and Email to send notifications based on your location (address taken at the registration time) and other promotional messages or news. We will use your registration information to create and manage your Karnataka Political News service. We may deactivate your account if we suspect that you use our App to commit fraudulent or illegal acts or if you violate our terms of use.


Your Rights

As a user, you have the right to take information regarding your personal account, including information that you've provided to us and information regarding each services of the app. You may ask for the correction / updating of your personal data at any time by contacting our customer service or by writing a mail to editor@KarnatakaPoliticalNews.com from your registered mail id.


Security to protect your information and data retention

Our security procedures mean that we may request proof of identity before we are able to disclose sensitive information to you. In the circumstances set out in this policy where Karnataka Political News passes your information to a third party, we will ensure that appropriate technical and organizational security measures are taken to secure processing of your data. This does not apply where we need to pass your information to a third party by law & we will retain it for some duration long as the law requires. However, sometime we cannot guarantee the security of our database, while being transmitted to us over the Internet. Any transmission of information by you to Karnataka Political News is at your own risk. We recommend that you do not disclose your account details to anyone.


Changes in This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically based on our information practices without prior notice to you. If we make any changes we will endeavour to notify by means of a notice on the Website prior to the change becoming effective. We may or may not notify you by email. We encourage you to periodically review this page for the latest information on our privacy practices.


Contact Details

If you have questions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at editor@karnatakapoliticalnews.com. And if we decide to change our Privacy Policy, we will notify those changes on this page about the information that we collect and its use.
